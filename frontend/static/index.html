Patient Admission & Management

Easily admit new patients and create detailed profiles for each one. Track personal information, admission dates, and assigned hospital wards. This functionality ensures smooth patient onboarding and gives healthcare professionals immediate access to essential patient data.

  • Effortlessly create and update patient profiles
  • Track patient admission dates and status
  • Real-time access to essential patient data
  • Enable healthcare teams to view patient information instantly

Treatment & Medication Tracking

Keep track of prescribed medications and treatments for each patient. Doctors can add and update treatment plans, ensuring that patients receive the correct medication and dosage at the right times.

  • Add and monitor medications, dosages, and schedules
  • Track treatment progress
  • Allow patients to confirm medication intake
  • Push reminders for medication adherence

Patient History & Reports

Access detailed patient history, including past treatments, surgeries, and test results. Doctors and patients can view progress and generate reports for further analysis or sharing with external systems.

  • View comprehensive patient medical history
  • Generate automated summaries and email notifications
  • Allow authorized personnel to access sensitive data securely
  • Track patient progress over time

Reusable Profiles & Multi-Admissions

Maintain patient profiles that can be reused across multiple hospital visits. Seamlessly manage recurring admissions, doctor assignments, and centralized patient interactions.

  • Maintain reusable patient profiles for recurring visits
  • Efficiently manage multiple admissions and discharges
  • Coordinate care assignments among doctors
  • Centralize records for long-term patient management
  • Provide seamless updates for ongoing treatments and follow-ups

Technologies We Use

Java & Spring Boot

Our backend is powered by Java and Spring Boot, delivering reliable and scalable server-side solutions that ensure robust and secure performance for modern web applications

Kotlin Multiplatform

With Kotlin Multiplatform, we embrace innovative and versatile technology that enables us to share code across platforms, ensuring faster development and consistent performance for Android and IOS

Docker

We rely on Docker to containerize our applications, making them portable and efficient, with seamless deployment and scalability across environments, including deploying containers on virtual machine

HOW IT WORKS

Follow These Simple Steps

  • 1
    Register the Patient

    The doctor creates a profile for the patient and opens a new medical case to document the patient’s condition and diagnosis. This ensures that all relevant information about the patient’s health is readily available and well-organized from the start

  • 2
    Track Treatment Progress

    Record prescribed medications, scheduled procedures, and treatment history in an easy-to-use interface, accessible by both doctors and patients. Updates are saved in real-time, providing a transparent and detailed view of the treatment journey for better collaboration

  • 3
    Get Visit Summaries

    Receive detailed summaries of your hospital visit, including diagnoses, treatments, and scheduled follow-ups. These summaries can be stored and updated seamlessly for future appointments, making recurring visits more efficient and stress-free
